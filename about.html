About me

I was born in Addis Ababa,Ethiopia in April 25,2000. I went to Omega school for kindergarten and went to Lideta Catholic Cathedral school for the past 12 years of my elementary and high school. I finished upto 6th grade in nativity girls school which were an only girls school which is in the same compound as the one I finished highschool in. So I received my diploma from there and I am so glad to call myself a cathenian.

Once upon a time a girl was attending in Addis Ababa information technology university. She got so stressed with school and assignments. Then she prayed “may some magic happen and let this be over”. Her prayer was heard and a flu cancelled school. It lasted more than 9 months where a baby can be made. In this 9 month she discovered herself and know what she wants to do for life. This girl wrote a book even if it’s a small one it was an eye opener. Her future so bright, a life with purpose.

In addition of this girl’s mini story, as a kid she always wanted to be a person who gives life skill speeches and courses and also wanted to share her sight of life and the world because she believes she has a good expressing of things skill. So she break the shell out she were in and created a YouTube channel.

Thank you so much for reading
